About Me (Curriculum Vitae)

Lei is now a first-year Ph.D. Student with the Department of Computer Science at The University of Hong Kong (HKU) since Sept. 2022, supervised by Prof. Ruibang Luo.

Prior to that, he received his M.Eng. degree in Computer Technology at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) , also with the affiliation of Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (SIAT, CAS), supervised by Prof. Min Yang.

His research interests lie in Bioinformatics, Natural Language Processing, Recommender Systems, etc.

He was so fortunate to have research internships at Representation Learning Lab, Westlake University under the supervision of Prof. Fajie Yuan, and WXG, Tencent with the 2021 Tencent Rhino-Bird Research Elite Training Program.
